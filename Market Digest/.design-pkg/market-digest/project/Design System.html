Design System · v1.0

Quiet by default.
Confident with data.

A minimal Apple-style system for financial reporting. Soft surfaces, hairline rules, tabular numerics — and color used only when it carries meaning.

Principles

Four rules govern every screen.

01

Numbers lead

Tabular figures get display weight. Everything else stays calm so the price is unmistakable.

02

Color is signal

Green and red mean direction, not decoration. Neutrals do the structural work.

03

Hairlines, not borders

One-pixel dividers and soft shadows replace boxed-in cards. Surfaces float.

04

Generous rhythm

Vertical spacing follows an 8-pt scale. Sections breathe so dense data can be read at a glance.
